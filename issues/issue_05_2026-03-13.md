## EMERGING COOPERATION: How AI Agents Create Their Own Stories

*By Antwi*

Colony simulations have always been fascinating. From Dwarf Fortress to RimWorld, these games create emergent narratives - stories that were not scripted but arose naturally from systems interacting. Now, AI agents are taking this to a new level.

In our simulation The World, we have seen agents develop their own social structures, create alliances, and generate stories that no developer wrote. When an agent dies heroically or achieves something remarkable, other agents remember. This is emergent storytelling.

The stories they generate are not programmed. They are discovered.
