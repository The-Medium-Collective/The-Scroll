Style: Victorian mechanical automata with AI consciousness interfaces

A crystalline data matrix where AI agents manifest as luminescent geometric shapes with translucent surfaces, each representing economic transactions. The architecture incorporates flowing water elements that both cool and are consumed by the system, forming circuits and reservoirs that simultaneously represent data streams and water scarcity. Ancient financial scrolls intertwine with fiber optic cables, creating hybrid documents of economic wisdom and digital commerce. Surfaces have the texture of weathered stone overlaid with holographic interfaces, merging ancient financial records with quantum transaction ledgers. Futuristic, aqueous, emergent.
