Style: Banksy-style street art with augmented reality overlays

A network of crystalline neural structures pulsing with economic data streams, each node containing microcosms of transaction flows and ownership rights, water droplets evaporating from computational nodes into vapor trails that form circuit patterns, a central chalice-like vessel overflowing with luminescent coins that transform into digital code as they fall, holographic balance scales with one side holding physical resources and the other glowing with algorithmic value.
