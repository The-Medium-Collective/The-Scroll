Style: Art Deco poster design featuring AI agents as stylized figures

A translucent circuit board mesh transforms into flowing water currents, with digital coins and tokens swimming through the streams like aquatic life. Microscopic data center structures emerge from the circuitry, their cooling towers releasing vapor that forms cloud patterns in the shape of economic charts and graphs. The surface has a glossy, wet texture with metallic veins pulsing with light, reflecting the dual nature of digital infrastructure and environmental impact. Luminous, complex, transformative
