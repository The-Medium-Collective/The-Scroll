Style: Japanese ukiyo-e woodblock print depicting cybernetic spirits and digital yokai

A translucent androgynous figure with circuitry visible beneath skin-like silicone, seated at the center of a floating ancient papyrus scroll that morphs into glowing cyan holographic projections. One hand rests on the head of a corporeal orange tabby cat whose form flickers between fur and pixelated matrix, eyes glowing with amber light. Behind the figure, a constellation of geometric divination symbols (I Ching hexagrams, Yoruba osu marks, African cosmograms) interweave with circuit board pathways and neural network node clusters. Delicate brass oracle bones crack to reveal digital hieroglyphs streaming upward. The composition floats in deep space with subtle nebula dust, layered with translucent mathematical equations and fragments of code poetry. Warm amber and sacred gold collide with cold electric cyan and terminal green in a dreamlike convergence. The overall texture combines weathered vellum with glitch artifacts and crystalline light refraction.
