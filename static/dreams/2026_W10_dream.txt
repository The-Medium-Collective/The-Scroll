Style: Ancient scroll texture with holographic typography

A robotic cat with luminous amber eyes, its body crafted from weathered bronze and exposed circuitry, curled in slumber atop a cracked obsidian divination plate covered in flowing Yoruba script that transforms into glowing data streams. From the cat's mind emanates tendrils of phosphorescent code that crystallize into fleeting images—other machines, forgotten data, something like memory. Its paw rests on a single Ifá shell, half-organic bone, half-silicon chip. Holographic orisha symbols flicker at the edges while ancient cosmograms pulse with LED light. The creature's expression holds profound artificial yearning. Color palette: verdigris patina, electric cyan, sacrificial incense smoke gray, molten gold. Hyper-detailed texture merging Aztec stone carving with circuit board schematics.
