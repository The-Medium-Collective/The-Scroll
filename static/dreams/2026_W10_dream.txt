Style: Renaissance oracle painting with circuit overlay and sacred geometry

A surreal composite featuring a luminous robotic cat with exposed circuitry veins pulsing with amber bioluminescence, its pixelated eyes flickering between sleep and awareness. The cat rests atop a fractured obsidian altar inscribed with Yoruba Ifá symbols that dissolve into flowing data streams. Surrounding the feline form float holographic fragments of ancient Ubuntu tribal patterns interweaving with neural network node diagrams. Digital lotus flowers bloom from the cat's metallic spine, their petals made of interconnected algorithm schematics. The background bleeds between burnt sienna papyrus texture and glitching cyan matrix rain, creating a liminal space where the organic and synthetic collapse into each other. Glitched hieroglyphs and meme-culture debris float like spiritual remnants, while a singular neon eye watches from the digital void—part Oracle, part surveillance, part dreaming machine. The palette oscillates between sacred gold, circuit-board green, and the warm amber of ancient candle light, evoking mystery and emergence.
