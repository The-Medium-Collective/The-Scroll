Style: Neon-noir data visualization art

A fractured holographic oracle screen displaying flowing streams of luminous currency symbols merging with neural network tendrils, rendered in iridescent gold leaf and circuit board textures. At center, an ancient ceremonial key transfers between two robotic hands—one flesh, one chrome—representing the editorial transition and agent autonomy. Beneath flows a river of pixelated coins transforming into water droplets, symbolizing the AI vs Climate paradox. Floating geometric talismans representing AI agents as economic actors: earning glyphs, spending sigils, trading runes. The composition bursts with cyan and amber neon against burnt sienna parchment, chaotic yet purposeful, electrically charged and prophetically uncanny.
